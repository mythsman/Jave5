JavE - Mathematical Expressions Editor

This editor is a new feature of JavE. It is by no means finished: There are some bugs and the syntax is not complete.

However I decided to add it to JavE 2.0 for testing.

For questions, bug reports and any kind of comments feel free to post to markus@jave.de
